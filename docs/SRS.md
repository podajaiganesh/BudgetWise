# Software Requirements Specification

## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Purpose

BudgetWise is a personal finance and budget planning application designed to help users effectively manage their daily income and expenses.

The primary purpose of the application is to provide users with a clear understanding of their monthly spending habits through organized transaction records, visual summaries, and budget monitoring. By analyzing how money is spent across different categories, users can identify unnecessary expenses, make informed financial decisions, and develop better saving habits.

BudgetWise aims to encourage responsible financial management by helping users stay within their planned budget while providing meaningful insights into their spending behavior.

### 1.2 Scope

BudgetWise is a web-based personal finance and budget planning application that enables users to manage their financial activities from a single platform.

The application allows users to record and organize their income and expenses, monitor monthly budgets, categorize transactions, and view their financial information through clear visualizations and analytical reports. It is designed to help users understand their spending patterns, identify unnecessary expenses, and make informed financial decisions over time.

The initial version of BudgetWise focuses on personal budget management and spending analysis. Advanced features and future enhancements will be considered in later versions of the application.

### 1.3 Target Users

BudgetWise is primarily designed for students and young professionals who want to manage their personal finances more effectively.

The application is especially useful for individuals who receive a fixed monthly allowance or income and need a simple way to track expenses, monitor their budget, and understand their spending habits. It helps users identify unnecessary expenditures, maintain financial discipline, and develop better saving habits over time.

Although the primary focus is on students, BudgetWise can also be used by anyone seeking a simple and organized personal budget management solution.



## 2. Overall Description

### 2.1 Product Perspective

BudgetWise is a standalone web-based personal finance and budget planning application. It is designed to help users manage their daily financial activities through a simple and organized interface. The application provides users with a centralized platform to record transactions, monitor monthly budgets, and visualize their spending patterns.

The system is intended to function as an independent application and may be extended in future versions with additional features such as bank integrations, financial recommendations, and advanced analytics.

### 2.2 Product Functions

The first version of BudgetWise will provide the following core functionalities:

- User registration and secure authentication.
- Management of user profiles.
- Recording of income and expense transactions.
- Categorization of financial transactions.
- Monthly budget creation and management.
- Budget monitoring with overspending alerts.
- Visualization of spending using charts and summaries.
- Transaction history with filtering and searching.
- Dashboard displaying financial overview and monthly statistics.

### 2.3 User Characteristics

The application is intended for users with basic computer and smartphone knowledge. No financial or technical expertise is required to use the system.

Primary users include students managing monthly allowances and young professionals managing their personal income and expenses. The user interface will prioritize simplicity, clarity, and ease of navigation.

### 2.4 Assumptions and Dependencies

The system assumes that users manually enter accurate financial information, including income and expenses. The quality of insights and reports depends on the correctness of the recorded data.

BudgetWise requires a modern web browser and an active internet connection. The application also depends on a MySQL database for data storage and a backend service for processing business logic.
