# Software Requirements Specification

## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Purpose

BudgetWise is a personal finance and budget planning application designed to help users effectively manage their daily income and expenses.

The primary purpose of the application is to provide users with a clear understanding of their monthly spending habits through organized transaction records, visual summaries, and budget monitoring. By analyzing how money is spent across different categories, users can identify unnecessary expenses, make informed financial decisions, and develop better saving habits.

BudgetWise aims to encourage responsible financial management by helping users stay within their planned budget while providing meaningful insights into their spending behavior.

### 1.2 Scope

BudgetWise is a web-based personal finance and budget planning application that enables users to manage their financial activities from a single platform.

The application allows users to record and organize their income and expenses, monitor monthly budgets, categorize transactions, and view their financial information through clear visualizations and analytical reports. It is designed to help users understand their spending patterns, identify unnecessary expenses, and make informed financial decisions over time.

The initial version of BudgetWise focuses on personal budget management and spending analysis. Advanced features and future enhancements will be considered in later versions of the application.
